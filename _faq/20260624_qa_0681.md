# Compliance Alert: What constitutes insider trading under Chinese securities law?

**问：** Compliance Alert: What constitutes insider trading under Chinese securities law?

**答：** Under Article 180 of the PRC Criminal Law, insiders who trade on material non-public information before disclosure, or disclose such information to others, face up to 5 years imprisonment for serious cases and 5-10 years for particularly serious cases. Fines range from 1x to 5x the illegal gains. Key elements include possession of material non-public information and trading before public disclosure.
