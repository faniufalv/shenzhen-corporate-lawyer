# Compliance Alert: How does China regulate illegal fundraising?

**问：** Compliance Alert: How does China regulate illegal fundraising?

**答：** The 2022 Supreme Court interpretation defines illegal fundraising as absorbing funds from unspecified public with promised returns without approval. Prosecution thresholds: individual 1 million RMB, 150+ victims, or 500,000 RMB direct losses. Regular private lending to specific individuals at rates below 4x LPR is legal. Companies should strictly limit fundraising scope to avoid criminal liability.
