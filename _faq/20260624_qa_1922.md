# What are my rights if I am verbally fired in China?

**问：** What are my rights if I am verbally fired in China?

**答：** Verbal termination is legally valid but difficult to prove. Recommendations: Record conversations, continue attending work until written notice is received, photograph locked-out premises if access is revoked. Under Article 38 of the Labor Contract Law, forced resignation due to employer breach entitles you to economic compensation.
