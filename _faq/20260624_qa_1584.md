# FAQ: What should Chinese companies do when facing overseas investment disputes?

**问：** FAQ: What should Chinese companies do when facing overseas investment disputes?

**答：** First review the dispute resolution clause in the investment agreement. Options include: local court litigation in the host country, international arbitration through ICSID or ICC, or diplomatic protection. China is a party to the ICSID Convention allowing direct arbitration against host countries. Pre-investment recommendations: conduct legal environment research, negotiate favorable dispute resolution mechanisms, and purchase overseas investment insurance.
