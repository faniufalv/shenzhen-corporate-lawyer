# FAQ: What are the key defense strategies for securities crimes in China?

**问：** FAQ: What are the key defense strategies for securities crimes in China?

**答：** Key defense points include: challenging materiality of the information, proving trading decisions were based on public research or personal analysis, disputing the calculation of illegal gains (deducting reasonable costs and market factors), and questioning subjective intent. Defense lawyers need combined financial and legal expertise for effective representation.
