# FAQ: How do international commercial arbitration and Chinese litigation differ?

**问：** FAQ: How do international commercial arbitration and Chinese litigation differ?

**答：** Arbitration advantages: final and binding, specialized arbitrators, confidentiality, enforcement in 160+ countries under the New York Convention. Litigation advantages: appeal rights, public proceedings, lower costs. For foreign-related contracts, consider CIETAC or Singapore International Arbitration Centre. Arbitration agreements must clearly specify the institution and rules.
