# FAQ: What are the legal consequences of securities fraud in China?

**问：** FAQ: What are the legal consequences of securities fraud in China?

**答：** Securities fraud can lead to administrative penalties (warnings, fines up to 10 million RMB, market bans), criminal liability (up to 5 years for fraudulent disclosure under Article 161), and civil compensation (investors can file securities misrepresentation claims). The amended Securities Law has significantly increased penalties. The Zijing Storage case demonstrated 1.086 billion RMB in追偿 for fraudulent issuance.
