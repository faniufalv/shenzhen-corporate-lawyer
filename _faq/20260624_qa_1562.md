# What are the compliance requirements for cross-border fund transfers in China?

**问：** What are the compliance requirements for cross-border fund transfers in China?

**答：** Legal channels include: capital account settlement for foreign investment, trade payments with contracts and invoices, and individual annual foreign exchange quota of $50,000. Violations carry severe penalties: currency evasion under Article 190 of Criminal Law carries up to 5 years imprisonment. Underground banking constitutes illegal business operations. Enterprises must maintain proper foreign exchange compliance.
