# Policy Update: Can I get severance pay if the company relocates and I refuse to move?

**问：** Policy Update: Can I get severance pay if the company relocates and I refuse to move?

**答：** Yes. Cross-city relocation constitutes a material change in circumstances under Article 40 of the PRC Labor Contract Law. If协商 fails, the employer may terminate but must pay economic compensation (N). Without proper consultation, termination constitutes illegal dismissal requiring 2N compensation. Same-city relocation generally does not qualify unless commute time increases significantly.
