# FAQ: What are common legal risks for cross-border e-commerce businesses?

**问：** FAQ: What are common legal risks for cross-border e-commerce businesses?

**答：** Key risks include: IP infringement leading to platform delisting or lawsuits, product non-compliance resulting in customs seizure, tax compliance issues, and cross-border data transfer requiring security assessment. Compliance essentials: register trademarks in target countries, ensure product certifications (CE, FDA), understand local consumer protection laws, and use compliant payment channels. Engage local legal counsel for each market.
