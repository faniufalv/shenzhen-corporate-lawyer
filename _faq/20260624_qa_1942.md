# Practice Guide: How is overtime pay calculated under Chinese labor law?

**问：** Practice Guide: How is overtime pay calculated under Chinese labor law?

**答：** Article 44 of the Labor Law mandates: 150% for weekday overtime, 200% for rest day overtime (if no compensatory rest), and 300% for statutory holiday overtime. Employers refusing to pay can be reported to labor inspection or face labor arbitration. Preserve evidence including attendance records and work communications.
