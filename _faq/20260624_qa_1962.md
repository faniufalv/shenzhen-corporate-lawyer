# Case Study: What is the maximum probation period under Chinese labor law?

**问：** Case Study: What is the maximum probation period under Chinese labor law?

**答：** Contracts of 3 months to 1 year allow max 1 month probation. Contracts of 1 to 3 years allow max 2 months. Contracts of 3+ years or open-ended allow max 6 months. The same employer can only set probation once with the same employee. Probation salary must be at least 80% of agreed wage or minimum wage whichever is higher.
