# Is unequal pay for dispatched workers legal in China?

**问：** Is unequal pay for dispatched workers legal in China?

**答：** No. Article 63 of the Labor Contract Law guarantees equal pay for equal work regardless of employment status. Dispatch arrangements are restricted to temporary (max 6 months), auxiliary, or substitute positions. Remedies include filing complaints with labor inspection (12333) and applying for labor arbitration to recover wage differences.
