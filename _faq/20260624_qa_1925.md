# What protections do gig workers have in China?

**问：** What protections do gig workers have in China?

**答：** The classification depends on the working model. Dedicated riders under platform control generally qualify as employees. Crowdsourced riders with more flexibility may be classified differently. The Ministry of Human Resources has issued guidelines requiring platforms to bear corresponding responsibilities for new employment forms. Preserve order records and income screenshots as evidence.
